Personal Projects

In this project, I created a flask application that allows the user to create an account and save reviews on movies they have read. The movie can be rated out of 10 and users can add their review on the movie. I used TheMovieDB API to add the details of the books such as the cover and author title. The users' data are also saved in a database so that when they log back in, their reviews will be retained.
